Studio Tools

Here you can find all of the information about Studio Tools!

'Studio Tools' is an unofficial 'Unity Engine' asset that was made by us to make your gaming career a bit smoother.

The asset itself will be uploaded to our website at the latest stable of unity at the time of the uploading and will be updated every once in a while;
the asset itself is still in development and will not release soon.
Furthermore, the asset will be completely free for download and we will provide you with an installation guide and the unity asset.

The asset will include:

-First Person Controller
-Third Person Controller
-Complete Damage and Defense System
-Enemy Prefab
-Control on every parameter of the scripts
-Change Log with every element being added
-Customized HUD
-etc

We must declare, this asset is free to use for anyone who seeks it, but we won't engage you to not only use this asset without coding skills. will it be possible to make a game without coding with the asset - mostly, but we do not recommend that at all and we engage you to learn at least the basics.
